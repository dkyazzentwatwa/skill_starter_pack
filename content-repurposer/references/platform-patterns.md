# Platform patterns

Use these quick patterns when drafting channel-native copy.

## Contrarian hook patterns

- Everyone optimizes for [surface metric]. Almost nobody fixes [real bottleneck].
- [Popular tactic] sounds smart. It usually fails because [reason].
- The problem is not [obvious culprit]. It is [less obvious cause].
- Most advice on [topic] is upside down.
- People think [belief]. In practice, [reality].

## Minimal cta patterns

- Subscribe for more essays like this.
- Reply with where this breaks.
- Share this with someone wrestling with it.
- More on this soon.
- No CTA needed.

## Carousel pacing

Use the fewest slides needed.

### 2-slide version
- Slide 1: hook
- Slide 2: insight or closing point

### 3-slide version
- Slide 1: hook
- Slide 2: reframe
- Slide 3: takeaway

### 4-6 slide version
- Slide 1: hook
- Slide 2: misconception
- Slide 3: reframe
- Slide 4: example or principle
- Slide 5: takeaway
- Slide 6: optional closing insight or minimal CTA
