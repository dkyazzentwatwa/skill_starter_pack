# SOP Review Checklist

Run this pass before finalizing the SOP.

## Structure

- Does the SOP clearly state purpose, scope, audience, owner, and trigger?
- Are required inputs, tools, and outputs visible?
- Is the procedure in chronological order?

## Operator usability

- Could a new team member run this without hidden tribal knowledge?
- Are vague phrases replaced with explicit actions?
- Are required steps clearly separated from optional best practices?
- Are decision points written as clear if/then branches?

## Quality control

- Does the SOP explain how to verify completion and quality?
- Are likely failure modes included?
- Are exceptions or edge cases handled where relevant?
- Is there a clear escalation path?

## Trustworthiness

- Are assumptions labeled instead of stated as fact?
- Are missing details called out plainly?
- Are conflicting instructions preserved rather than silently merged?

## Maintenance

- Are revision notes included or ready to fill in?
- Would future workflow changes be easy to update in this structure?

## Final smell test

- Does this read like a runnable operating procedure rather than polished notes?
- Did unnecessary policy language get cut?
- Is the SOP concise without hiding important details?
